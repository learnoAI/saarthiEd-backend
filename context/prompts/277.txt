**OUTPUT FORMAT REQUIREMENT (STRICT):**


**1. Question Field:**
- Begin with the label (e.g., "Q1.", "Q2.").

- List all printed numbers separated by punctuation as seen in the image.
- **No** newline characters `\n` in the value.

- **Detection:** Look for handwritten digits, **circled numbers**, or underlined numbers.

- **Ambiguity:** Distinguish carefully between **4** and **8**, **1** and **7**. If unsure, choose the digit that matches a number available in the question list.

**3. Formatting:**
- `question_number`: Integer.
- `student_answer`: String (e.g., "5"). If absolutely no mark is found, use `""`.
